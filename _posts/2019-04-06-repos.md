Public repositories on [`GitHub`](https://github.com/garrethmartin) — excluding forks.

### [`lsst_latam_lsb_workshop_2025`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/lsst_latam_lsb_workshop_2025)
Materials for low-surface-brightness imaging workshop held at LSST:LatAM 2025 meeting in Mexico

### [`Leiden_sims_workshop`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/Leiden_sims_workshop)
Simulations hand-on session held at Lorentz Centre workshop "Intra-Cluster Light: Bridging the Gap Between Theory and Observations"

### [`make_mocks`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/make_mocks)
A Python package for generating mock galaxy images and IFU cubes, specifically for low surface brightness imaging

### [`interactive-profiles-ICL`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/interactive-profiles-ICL)
interactive plots for ICL/DM density profiles

### [`python_fits_viewer`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/python_fits_viewer)
view and mask .fits images

### [`progenitor-fractions`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/progenitor-fractions)
Returns the joint progenitor probability (probability that a galaxy is a spheroid at z=0) for a given redshift, mass, environment (percentile) and star-formation rate.

### [`MBEtree`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/MBEtree)
Efficient implementation of modified Breiman density estimator [Brieman et al. (1977), WIlkinson and Meijer (1995)] using balltree method

### [`bh_potential`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/bh_potential)
computes potentials for a group of particles using Barnes-Hut octree method

### [`HSC_UML`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/HSC_UML)
Clusters objects found in a list of astronomical images by their visual similarity. 

### [`smooth3d`](https://github.com/garrethmartin/smooth3d)
smooths 3d distribution based on distance to nearest neighbour
